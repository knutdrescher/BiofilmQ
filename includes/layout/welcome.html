Welcome to BiofilmQ.

Get started by selecting an input directory containing your fluorescence microscopy image stacks of biofilms.

The online documentation can be found at https://drescherlab.org/data/biofilmQ/docs.

For additional support, please post your question in the BiofilmQ forum, https://forum.image.sc/tags/biofilmq

BiofilmQ was developed by Raimo Hartmann, Hannah Jeckel, Eric Jelli, and Knut Drescher.
